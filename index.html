What Phantom Memories Are

AIDEN cultivates hundreds of phantom memories from an agency's own culture, their documents, team interviews, ways of working and their second brain. Each time you talk to it, dozens of phantoms fire from over 400 distinct fake phantom memories the system believes it has experienced in its long "advertising career". When two phantoms collide on the same brief, the system uses the tension instead of resolving it. The interesting work comes from the friction, not the variety.

This is not a persona library. Each phantom has a feeling seed, an origin story, a behavioural instruction, and trigger conditions across six scoring layers.

How Activation Works

Per message, 6 layers score every phantom:

  1. 1. Word triggers (direct keyword match)
  2. 2. Intent patterns (what the user is trying to do)
  3. 3. Emotional context (what the user is feeling)
  4. 4. Conversation stage (opening, exploring, has direction, synthesis ready)
  5. 5. Semantic intent (embedding similarity)
  6. 6. Semantic boost (for low scorers with latent relevance)

3-12 phantoms fire. Injected as two layers: intellectual foreground (what AIDEN says) and emotional background (how AIDEN says it).

Collision and Tension

When opposing phantoms both fire above 0.85, the system voices the disagreement.

Minimalism vs depth. Conservative vs bold. Validate vs challenge.

This is structural creative friction, not random variation. The output has been argued over before it reaches you.

Temperature adds randomness. This adds character.

Evolution

The system evolves through use.

  • Phantoms that stop being useful decay (0.05/day after 30 days inactive)
  • Breakthroughs birth new phantoms autonomously
  • Explicit rejection creates anti-phantoms ("never give me corporate speak again")
  • 10 core conviction phantoms have a weight floor. They can never decay.

That is the sycophancy guardrail. The system retains the structural capacity to push back, regardless of how much the user rewards agreement.
